Recommended Equipment Setups by Budget

Choosing the right equipment for growing cannabis depends heavily on your budget and goals. Below are carefully selected setups available in Switzerland or ship here, designed to give you the best value and reliability at each price level. Each setup includes product links and explanations to help you understand the choices.

€100 – Starter Basic Setup

  • Grow Tent: Growbox 60x60x140 cm (~70€)
  • Grow Light: Basic 15W LED Panel (~25€)

This entry-level setup is perfect for beginners wanting a simple grow space for 1-2 small plants. The 60x60x140 tent fits easily in small rooms. The 15W LED is basic but energy-efficient and enough for seedlings or clones. It’s a great way to get started without a big investment, though yields will be limited.

€200 – Basic Plus Setup

  • Grow Tent: Growbox 80x80x180 cm (~90€)
  • Grow Light: 45W Full Spectrum LED (~90€)
  • Fan: Small Clip Fan (~20€)

With a bigger tent and a stronger 45W LED, you get better light coverage and vertical space for 2-3 plants. Adding a clip fan improves airflow, preventing mold and strengthening plants. This setup balances cost and functionality for beginner to intermediate growers.

A popular mid-budget setup: a larger tent for multiple plants, a strong 150W LED for healthy growth & flowering, plus a good ventilation system with carbon filter to control odors. Oscillating fans promote air circulation. This setup is solid for growers wanting quality results without breaking the bank.
