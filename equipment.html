€100 and Below – Basic Starter Setup

This ultra-budget setup is designed for hobbyists who want to start small. It includes a compact tent and a simple LED lamp, enough for a tiny plant or cloning.

Products:
  • Secret Jardin Dark Room DR30 Tent (30x30x60 cm) – Compact and affordable, ideal for beginners.
  • ZKL CFL 60W LED Grow Lamp – Low power, basic lighting suitable for seedlings and clones.

Why this setup? It keeps costs extremely low but offers the essentials to get started. The small tent keeps the environment controlled while the low wattage LED uses minimal electricity. No ventilation included at this level, so ensure room airflow.
