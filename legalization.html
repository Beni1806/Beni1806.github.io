Legalization Status in Switzerland

The topic of cannabis legalization is gaining momentum in Switzerland. As of now, recreational use remains illegal, but regulated pilot projects in several cities are already underway to test the impacts of legal sales.

In the summer of 2025, the Federal Council is expected to submit a new proposal for cannabis legalization to public consultation (Vernehmlassung). This is a major step toward a possible change in national law.

The proposed legislation would allow regulated sale and personal use of cannabis for adults, under strict health and safety conditions. The goal is to reduce the black market, increase control over product quality, and better protect public health.

We will keep you updated here with the latest developments, political debates, and timelines.
