Current Legalization Status

Switzerland is moving toward cannabis legalization, with pilot projects and public debates ongoing. The government is considering regulated sales to adults, aiming to reduce illegal markets and improve public health control.

Laws may vary by canton, so always check local regulations. We will keep this section updated with the latest developments.

Growing Equipment by Budget

Here’s an overview of growing equipment combinations based on common budget ranges (in CHF):

  • 100 CHF Budget: Basic starter kit Includes a small grow tent (~60x60x120 cm), a 40W LED lamp, and basic ventilation (small clip fan). Ideal for beginners growing 1-2 plants.
  • 200 CHF Budget: Improved ventilation and lighting Tent (~80x80x160 cm), 100W full-spectrum LED lamp, clip fan, and carbon filter for odor control. Better plant growth and air quality.
  • 300 CHF Budget: Mid-level setup Larger tent (~100x100x200 cm), 150W LED grow light, inline exhaust fan with carbon filter, oscillating fan, and basic humidity controller.
  • 400 CHF Budget: Advanced beginner setup 120x120x200 cm tent, 200W+ LED grow light, quality inline fan + carbon filter combo, oscillating fan, digital humidity and temperature controller, and quality pots/soil.

Tips & Tricks for Growing Cannabis

  • Lighting: Use full-spectrum LED lights for energy efficiency and good plant development.
  • Ventilation: Proper airflow prevents mold and strengthens plants.
  • Watering: Avoid overwatering; let the soil dry slightly between waterings.
  • Nutrients: Use quality fertilizers designed for cannabis during vegetative and flowering stages.
  • Training: Techniques like LST (Low Stress Training) improve yield by maximizing light exposure.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overwatering, causing root rot.
  • Ignoring temperature and humidity control.
  • Using incorrect nutrients or overfeeding.
  • Neglecting ventilation and odor control.
  • Planting too many plants in a small tent.
